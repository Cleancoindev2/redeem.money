How rDAI works

The following should help you understand how rDAI works.

Remember rDAI is an ERC20 token, so almost everything is done in your own wallet of choice
You will only need to interact with our dApp (or the contracts) for specific actions

This explanation covers most of the functionality of rTokens and rDAI, although we have kept out some of the complications caused by compounding.
Want to discuss? Contact us!

Getting rDAI

Bob opens the rDAI app. He transfers 100 DAI to the contract.
100 rDAI are minted and credited to Bob's account.
The 100 DAI are invested at the best rate on selected DeFi lending protocols.
