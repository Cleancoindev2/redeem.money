Call for help

rDAI is a self funded project with no business model. If you think you can help with the project in any way, please get in touch

While we strive to build our software as securely as possible, and have done extensive testing on all funcionality, Solidity development is tricky, and we don't intend to publish to mainnet until our contracts are properly audited. Want to assist with the audit? Please get in touch!

We have great plans for rTokens and we are hoping to bring a sustainable funding model to many organizations in Ethereum.
